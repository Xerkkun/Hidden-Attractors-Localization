# F7 Method Comparison

F7 compares available Lyapunov results and F5 diagnostics by case. It preserves
method applicability, validation status, missing case-specific results, and
documented discrepancies.

The comparison does not certify chaos or hiddenness. DK2018 block-restart,
full-history Caputo QR, Fischer published GS, and the experimental QR lane
remain separate contracts.
